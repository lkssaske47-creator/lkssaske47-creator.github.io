Solution for display over other apps feature not available || 💯🔥 Display over other apps problem


Hey, everyone today we are fixing the display over other apps feature not available error experienced by most android users. Before we start you need to download app ops and shizuku app from the Google play store. You can also download them from APK pure incase they are missing in your store. Now with that said let's dive into the tutorial.
STEPS
Go to your phone settings and locate the about option, it's mainly located in the system settings. Look for the build number and tap on it until the developer option is enabled. Go back and open the developer option. Scroll down and look for wireless debugging option and enable it. You need to be connected to a wi-fi connection in order to proceed with this. Go back and open the app ops you downloaded earlier. Allow the shizuku option and press continue.Then click on open Shizuku. Click on the pairing option. You will advance and get a developer option. Click on it. Scroll down to wireless debugging and press just beside it like you are pressing the words. Select pair with pairing code. Fill the code provided in the pop up notification. And it should say pairing successful. Once done go back and press start. In some cases it's says try closing and opening the wireless debugging. But in some case it will just proceed. If it does that press on the manage options, scroll down to wireless debugging then turn it off and then immediately turn it on and then go back. The code press will automatically start and connect both apps. When done go back and open App ops. All the apps in your device will be processed. Here you can now access all the apps with developers options turned off and enable it. Just click on the app scroll and look for the display over other apps and allow it. Don't make it default it will be turned off automatically so just allow it for all the apps. So that's how to fix the display over other features not found error in your phone.
